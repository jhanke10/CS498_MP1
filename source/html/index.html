The Game of Soccer

About

Soccer is a sport that consists of teams of 11 players per side. The objective of the games is to try to get the soccer ball into the opposing teams net. Each player has a position on their respective teams. The winner is determined by the number of goals scored in one 90 minute game.

The Field

Rules

Start

Each team must start on their own respective side. 11 players must be on the field at the start of the game. Kick-off begins when the ball is kicked forward across the halfway line.

Play

There are two 45 minute halves. Players can pass, dribble, or shoot at the opposing teams goal. There are attackers and defenders for each team. Attackers try to score the ball, while defenders try to prevent being scored on.

Foul

There are yellow cards for minor fouls such as hard tackles or hard shoves. Red cards are given when the foul is severe or when a player gets two yellow cards.
